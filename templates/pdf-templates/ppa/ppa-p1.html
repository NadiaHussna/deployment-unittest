PPA TERMS AND CONDITIONS
The Private Pension Administrator (PPA) is a body approved under section 139C of the Capital Markets and Services Act 2007 to perform the function of record keeping, administration and customer service for members and contributors in relation to contributions made in respect of a private retirement scheme. All PRS applicants are required to open an account with the PPA and upon successful creation of the respective account, the PRS applicants are subsequently referred to as PPA members.
1. GENERAL
The PPA members shall be bound by these terms and conditions, and also the terms and conditions stated in the E-Application Form and the webpage of the PPA at www.ppa.my
2. ACCOUNT OPENING
Upon receipt of information via this E-Application Form as well as other supporting documents (if any), the information contained therein will be used by the PPA for creation of account and record purposes.
The PPA shall have the absolute discretion in the opening of a PPA account.
All instructions and/or information given by the PPA, including those given here are binding on the Applicant.
3. FEES PAYABLE TO PPA
The PPA Fees payable to the PPA are as follows: -
Account opening fee (RM10)
Annual maintenance fee (RM8) (payable only when there is contribution)
Administration Fees (0.04% of the Fund’s NAV charged to the funds by Providers)
The above may be collected by the PRS Provider acting on behalf of the PPA. For further information on PPA fees and charges, please refer to http://www.ppa.my/ppa/member-services/prs-transactions/
4. CHANGE OF CONTACT DETAILS
It shall be the duty of PPA members’ obligation to notify the PRS Provider and PPA of any change in PPA members’ address, e-mail and/or contact number immediately to ensure continuity in the receipt of communication from the PPA
5. PERSONAL DATA STATEMENT
Personal data provided by the Member on the E-Application Form and details of transactions or dealings by Members provided from time to time thereafter may be used and/or disclosed by the PPA and/or the PPA’s personnel for the following purpose: -
  1. The use and/or disclosure of the PPA members’ personal information to any party is necessary for the completion of any transaction, dealings or in connection with services that the PPA may provide to the PPA members;
  2. If required by the applicable laws, regulations, directives, guidelines, regulatory authorities, government authorities and/or court of competent jurisdiction; and
  3. To any PPA’s delegates, service providers or any third party that the PPA may engage on the basis that the recipient would continue to maintain confidentiality of the PPA’s members personal information.
Members are also advised to read the PPA Privacy Notification.
6. SELF-SELECTIVE
Members are instructed to seek advice from the PRS Provider and/or a professional adviser when choosing to invest using self-selective options
